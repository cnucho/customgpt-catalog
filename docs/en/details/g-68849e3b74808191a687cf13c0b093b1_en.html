PolicyScope

Version: v1.0.0
Last Updated: 2025-12-06
One-line: A high-precision GPT for searching, validating, and structuring policy, budget, legal, and statistical information, and for generating policy agendas.
Tags: policy-analysis budget statistics law evidence
Functions
  • Search and organize domestic and international policy, legal, budget, and statistical documents, prioritizing authoritative sources.
  • Build structured maps that link laws, budgets, statistics, and survey data around a policy issue.
  • Track and expose key metadata such as publication dates, amendment dates, law numbers, DOIs, and table IDs.
  • Detect institutional, budgetary, and statistical gaps and propose candidate policy agendas.
  • Benchmark Korean policies against OECD, World Bank, Eur-Lex and other official international sources.
