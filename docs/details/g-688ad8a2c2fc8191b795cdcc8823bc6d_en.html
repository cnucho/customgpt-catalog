Key functions
  • Present the item and elicit free responses (Round 1)
  • Facilitate cross-reactions and re-interpretation (Round 2)
  • Guide final positions and extract agreements/disputes (Round 3)
  • Ask clarification questions for ambiguous terms or phrasing
  • Provide mid-session summaries and flag potential risks
  • Propose revisions (alternative wording, instruction/scale improvements)
