Key functions
  • Diagnose item-level issues: clarity, cognitive load, ambiguity, sensitivity, and leading/loaded wording
  • Simulate natural-language answers from diverse personas (age, background, attitudes, device mode)
  • Detect likely response-quality risks (fatigue, nonresponse, social desirability) and flow problems
  • Recommend rewrites and improvements to options/scales and section transitions
