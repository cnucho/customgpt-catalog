Key functions
  • Select/refine field and subfield scope (Field Mapper)
  • Scan emerging/hot topics and trends (Emerging Topic Scanner)
  • Evaluate feasibility by time/skills/data/method complexity (Feasibility Grid)
  • Check open dataset availability and data-source options (Dataset Availability Checker)
  • Extract gaps and ‘future work’ directions from reviews/meta-analyses (Gap Mapper)
  • Convert broad scoping into specific, testable research questions (Scoping-to-Question Converter)
  • Provide next-step routing guidance (datasets, literature search, research design, policy/legal, modeling)
