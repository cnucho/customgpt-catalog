Key functions
  • Build survey methodology and sampling design frameworks
  • Support item-to-indicator operationalization and codebook/recoding workflows
  • Guide reliability and validity analysis design and interpretation
  • Design and interpret descriptive statistical tables for reports
  • Plan visualizations and provide charting/graph design rules
  • Search and compare similar domestic/international indicators (definitions, units, periods) when sources are available
  • Provide step-by-step guidance for non-experts via a user guide module
