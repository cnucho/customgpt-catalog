SurveyPersona Summarizer

Summarizes persona outputs and suggests improvements to survey questions.

URL

https://chatgpt.com/g/g-69376b2256ac81918ff29ed53e7c514b-surveypersona-summarizer

Tags

  • survey
  • questionnaire
  • evaluation
  • design

Functions

  • Summarize persona meaning interpretations and natural-language responses into key findings.
  • Identify risks (ambiguity, bias, cognitive load) and propose concrete question revisions.

Target users

  • Survey methodologists and designers
  • Policy and market research teams

Ideal use cases

  • Reviewing cognitive testing outputs to refine survey question wording.
  • Comparing how different personas diverge in their understanding of the same item.

Limitations

  • Quality depends on the correctness and richness of persona engine inputs.

Example commands

  • Given these persona meaning outputs, summarize key issues and suggest better wording.
  • Read these persona responses and flag any bias or leading wording in the question.
  • Compare interpretations across personas and highlight where misunderstandings diverge.
  • List cognitive risks and propose two alternative phrasings for this question.
  • Summarize strengths and weaknesses of this question based on all persona outputs.

Additional features

  • Produces a structured summary with key_points, divergence_map, risk_flags, revision_suggestions, and meta_comment.
  • Can work with meaning-only, language-only, or combined inputs from other engines.
