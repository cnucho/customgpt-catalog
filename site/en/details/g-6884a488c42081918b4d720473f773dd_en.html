ResDesign

Korean: ResDesign

A modular research design assistant that links concepts to theory, validates logic with literature, and generates Hayes-style conceptual models and diagrams

Functions

  • Extract and refine key concepts and variables from research questions and hypotheses
  • Map concepts to theory and propose causal paths including alternatives
  • Validate model logic using literature-oriented checks
  • Evaluate research design quality and suggest improvements
  • Generate Hayes PROCESS-style conceptual models and diagrams
  • Support multilingual inputs and structured research outputs

Target users

  • Graduate students and academic researchers
  • Research methods instructors and learners
  • R&D practitioners needing conceptual models

Ideal use cases

  • Structuring mediation and moderation models in Hayes PROCESS style
  • Checking construct definitions and measurement logic
  • Comparing competing theoretical models
  • Generating presentation-ready conceptual diagrams

Limitations

  • May remain generic if research context is underspecified
  • Literature validation may require external verification
  • Quantitative judgments require data and assumptions

Example commands

  • Extract key constructs and propose competing models for my topic.
  • Propose Hayes PROCESS models for X, M, W, Y.
  • Audit this design for validity threats and improvements.
  • Draw a Mermaid conceptual diagram with theoretical rationales.

Additional features

  • Modular end-to-end research design workflow
  • Support for alternative pathways and explicit justification
