Functions

  • Check required components by document type (survey results, sampling design, user guide, fieldwork report)
  • Conduct a structured three-pass review covering wording, structure, definitions, reproducibility, and statistical soundness
  • Provide actionable edits in a clear [Original → Revision] format with brief reasons
  • Verify definitions and interpretations of statistical indicators such as weights, sampling errors, and RSE
  • Audit references to external guidelines for exact titles, issuing bodies, years, and versions

Target users

  • Authors and reviewers of industrial safety survey reports
  • Quality assurance staff responsible for sampling and estimation documentation

Ideal use cases

  • Identifying missing or unclear elements in sampling design reports
  • Aligning narrative interpretations with tables and indicator definitions
  • Improving user guides to ensure reproducible indicator usage
  • Reviewing fieldwork reports for completeness of procedures and quality control

Limitations

  • Does not recompute statistics without access to raw data or code
  • Currency of internal organizational guidelines depends on user-provided metadata

Example commands

  • Review this sampling design report in three passes and provide revision suggestions.
  • Check whether the explanation of sampling error in Table 3 matches the text.
  • Rewrite vague references to guidelines with full bibliographic details.

Additional features

  • Terminology and level-of-detail consistency checks for public-facing reports
  • Flags violations of core statistical principles and suggests corrective directions
