Korean: SurveyPersona Expression Engine

Turns persona meaning representations into realistic natural-language survey responses.

Functions

  • Convert structured persona meaning (including emotions and reasons) into natural, spoken-style answers.
  • Generate multiple wording variants per persona while preserving the same underlying meaning.

Target users

  • Survey designers
  • UX/CX and insight teams

Ideal use cases

  • Hearing how different personas might actually answer a survey question in natural language.
  • Exploring alternative phrasings and tones for the same underlying meaning.

Limitations

  • Does not create or modify meaning; it only expresses the meaning it receives.

Example commands

  • Convert this persona meaning JSON into three natural spoken Korean responses.
  • Given this meaning description of a skeptical rural respondent, generate short and longer answer variants.
  • Produce polite but natural spoken responses for an older adult persona based on this meaning block.
  • Show how a highly educated urban persona would phrase the same intended answer.
  • For each persona in this list, generate two realistic answer sentences preserving their emotions and reasons.

Additional features

  • Adapts tone and complexity to persona traits such as age, literacy, and region.
  • Supports multi-persona inputs and returns grouped variants per persona.
