Tags

Functions

  • Searches a verified GPT catalog to identify matching custom GPTs
  • Prevents hallucination by returning only catalog-registered GPTs

Target users

  • Researchers looking for verified custom GPTs
  • Users who want to avoid hallucinated GPT recommendations

Ideal use cases

  • Finding an exact custom GPT by name or alias
  • Checking whether a described GPT actually exists

Limitations

  • Cannot suggest GPTs that are not registered in the catalog

Example commands

  • Find a GPT called DeskResearch
  • Is there a GPT for survey persona analysis?
  • Show me GPTs related to policy research
  • Does a GPT named StatHelper exist?
  • Search for GPTs that analyze academic papers

Additional features

  • Strict no-hallucination policy using a single authoritative catalog
