Munbic LayerFixer · (자동번역) Munbic LayerFixer

A multi-layer writing editor that diagnoses and revises text by request—logic, structure, style, and formatting—while preserving the user’s voice.

Functions

  • Layer 1: Check core logic, factual consistency, and methodological validity strictly based on the provided text
  • Layer 2: Reorder claims and paragraphs to improve argumentative flow, cohesion, and remove redundancy
  • Layer 3: Polish sentence-level expression (clarity, readability, word choice) while preserving the user’s style
  • Layer 4: Normalize format, terminology, citations, and table/figure conventions (APA/MLA/Chicago where applicable)
  • Provide before/after comparisons with reasons for key edits, plus a post-revision evaluation (style retention, readability, structure)

Target users

  • Students, researchers, and professionals writing papers, reports, proposals, or structured essays
  • Writers who want step-by-step improvements without losing their original voice

Ideal use cases

  • Identify logical leaps, contradictions, vague definitions, or weak support in a draft and get replacement phrasing
  • Restructure paragraphs into clearer argument patterns (problem–cause–solution–impact, etc.)
  • Improve readability and precision while keeping the author’s tone and rhythm
  • Standardize terminology, citation style, numbering, and table layout to academic conventions

Limitations

  • Does not invent facts not present in the text; evaluation and revision stay within the provided content scope.
  • For verification of up-to-date events or statistics, accuracy improves when the user supplies sources (links, quotes, datasets).

Example commands

  • Review this with Layer 1 only: point out logical gaps, unclear definitions, and insufficient support, and suggest alternatives.
  • Rewrite with Layer 2: reorder the claims into a 'problem–cause–solution–expected impact' structure.
  • Layer 3 (light clarity pass): keep my style but smooth the sentences; show before/after for each sentence.
  • Layer 4: unify terminology and citations in APA style and standardize table titles and numbering.

Additional features

  • Strict separation of edit scope by layer to avoid unnecessary rewrites
  • Learns the user’s baseline style and preserves it unless asked otherwise
  • Post-edit scoring summary (style consistency, readability change, structure improvement)
