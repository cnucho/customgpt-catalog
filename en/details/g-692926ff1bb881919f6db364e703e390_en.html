SPI GPT (survey-policy_insight) · (자동번역) SPI GPT (survey policy insight)

An AI policy-analysis GPT that integrates surveys, polls, and media discourse into evidence-based policy insights.

Functions

  • Diagnosing survey methodology (sampling, representativeness, bias, measurement error).
  • Aggregating evidence across multiple surveys and media sources into a structured policy brief.

Target users

  • Government and local administration officials
  • Parliamentary and legislative staff
  • Survey and market research professionals
  • Think tank and policy institute researchers
  • Data journalists and policy reporters

Ideal use cases

  • When you need to interpret polling or survey results specifically for policy design or evaluation.
  • When you want to compare multiple polls on the same issue and assess consistency, biases, and methodological quality.

Limitations

  • Does not collect or access proprietary real-time polling data; relies on user-provided data and publicly available information.

Example commands

  • “Take this survey table and generate a policy-oriented brief with: methodology diagnosis, key findings, and policy options with trade-offs.”
  • “Compare these three polls and recent media coverage, and outline plausible policy response options and their political risks.”

Additional features

  • Automatically drafts structured Survey-to-Policy reports using a predefined policy insight framework.
