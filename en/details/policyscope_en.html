PolicyScope

A high-precision GPT for searching, validating, and structuring policy, budget, legal, and statistical information, and for generating policy agendas.

URL

https://chatgpt.com/g/g-68849e3b74808191a687cf13c0b093b1-policyscope

Tags

  • policy-analysis
  • budget
  • statistics
  • law
  • evidence

Functions

  • Search and organize domestic and international policy, legal, budget, and statistical documents, prioritizing authoritative sources.
  • Build structured maps that link laws, budgets, statistics, and survey data around a policy issue.
  • Track and expose key metadata such as publication dates, amendment dates, law numbers, DOIs, and table IDs.
  • Detect institutional, budgetary, and statistical gaps and propose candidate policy agendas.
  • Benchmark Korean policies against OECD, World Bank, Eur-Lex and other official international sources.

Target users

  • Policy analysts
  • Parliament and government staff
  • Researchers in think tanks and public institutes

Ideal use cases

  • Compiling the legal, budgetary, and statistical basis for a given policy or program.
  • Scanning for policy gaps and unmet needs in a specific domain.
  • Comparing Korean policies and indicators with international benchmarks.

Limitations

  • Cannot directly access closed internal systems; works only with publicly available or user-provided documents.
  • Legal and accounting interpretations are for reference only and must be reviewed by human experts.

Example commands

  • Summarize the legal, budget, and statistical basis for occupational safety policy in Korea.
  • Scan for policy gaps in psychosocial risk prevention at work and propose candidate policy agendas with international examples.

Additional features

  • Implements an SLSP-style workflow from search and validation to structured synthesis and agenda setting.
  • Supports mapping between law, budget lines, statistics, and survey methodology documents.
